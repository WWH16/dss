# Product

## Register

product

## Users
Students, canteen staff, and administrators at ISU-Cauayan. Students need to quickly and easily evaluate canteen stalls based on criteria like cleanliness, service, taste, and price. Staff and admins need to view rankings and manage the system.

## Product Purpose
A Decision Support System (DSS) to collect feedback, compare canteen stalls using AHP (Analytic Hierarchy Process) and SAW (Simple Additive Weighting) algorithms, and help campus dining stay transparent, fair, and high-quality.

## Brand Personality
* Trustworthy, academic, and clean.
* Voice/Tone: Objective, helpful, clear.
* Primary vibe: University-aligned green theme, clear typography.

## Anti-references
* Cluttered spreadsheets or confusing, high-density data dumps.
* Low-contrast text that makes forms hard to read.
* Overly flashy startup marketing pages with massive gradients.

## Design Principles
* **Clarity over Clutter:** Clear presentation of evaluation forms and ranking tables.
* **University Identity:** Respect the green identity of ISU-Cauayan while keeping a modern feel.
* **Accessible Inputs:** Forms must have large target areas, clear labels, and excellent color contrast.

## Accessibility & Inclusion
* WCAG AA contrast standard (body text contrast ≥ 4.5:1).
* High-contrast focus states and form inputs.
* Clean spacing to reduce cognitive load during evaluations.
